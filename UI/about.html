How To Use iReporter

Create an account on this site using this link and start reporting what ever that you think needs the government intervention in your locality or even at the country level. This entails corruption activities, crimes among other pressing issues.

Whatever that you report is kept confidential. You can add more evidence like images or even vidoes to prove your point and then the matter shall be investigated making sure that the issues are resolved.

Users are required to indicate the location of the 'suspicious' activity either by selecting a point on the map or by entering the longitudes and latitudes pin pointing the location of the activity.
